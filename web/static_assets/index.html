Our Story

Six Pack Innovations was founded one afternoon by four friends who were sitting in a breakout room working on an assignment. As they brainstormed ideas, they discovered they all had a shared love of beer, bears, and puns. They decided to start a company that would bring together their passions and create innovative solutions for the world. And so, Six Pack Innovations was born! Our team of beer-loving bear enthusiasts is dedicated to finding creative solutions to everyday problems, all while cracking terrible puns along the way. So join us on this wild and hoppy ride, and let's make the world a better place, one brew at a time!
